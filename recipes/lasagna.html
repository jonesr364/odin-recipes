Lasagna

Description

This recipe will create the worlds best, greatest and most tasteful Lasagna you have ever had th pleasure of masticating

Ingredients

  • Precooked Microwave Lasagna
  • a block of cheese

Steps

  1. Open the lasagna packet
  2. Place Lasagna in the Microwave
  3. Cook as per intructions
  4. Remove and grate all the cheese
  5. Masticate
